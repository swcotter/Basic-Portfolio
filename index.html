About Me

Hello everyone and welcome to my portfolio. My name is Sam Cotter and I was born and raised in Seattle, Washington. I graduated from Landmark College in 2012 with an Associates Degree in Business Administration. I graduated from Southern Oregon University in 2017 with a Bachelors Degree in Business Administration with a Minor in Philosophy.

When I graduated from college in 2017 I had no real idea what I wanted to do with my life. I tried starting my own business, which failed. I spent some time working for my dad, and working for the car sharing service Zipcar before I got my current job at BMW Seattle.

In my free time, I enjoy working on my car, building models, flying my drone, and spending time with my family. I like going up to my family’s vacation house on Camano Island to either get away to get important work done, or to just sleep.

I am excited to get started and to show how ready I am to become a developer.
